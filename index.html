About V & W VOLKSWAGEN Foreign Car Repair

Welcome to V & W VOLKSWAGEN Foreign Car Repair, your trusted partner for professional automotive service in Murray, Utah. Under the expert ownership of Mark, we specialize in comprehensive repair and maintenance services for Volkswagen and other foreign vehicles.

Our commitment to excellence, combined with decades of experience and state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment, ensures that your vehicle receives the highest quality care. We treat every car as if it were our own, delivering honest, reliable service that keeps you safe on the road.

Whether you need routine maintenance, complex repairs, or diagnostic services, our certified technicians have the expertise to handle all your foreign car needs.

Our Services

We provide comprehensive automotive repair and maintenance services for all foreign vehicles, with specialized expertise in Volkswagen models.

Engine Diagnostics

Advanced computerized diagnostics to identify and resolve engine issues quickly and accurately.

Brake Service

Complete brake system inspection, repair, and replacement to ensure your safety on the road.

Transmission Repair

Expert transmission diagnostics, repair, and maintenance for manual and automatic transmissions.

Oil Changes

Regular oil change services using quality oils and filters to keep your engine running smoothly.

Electrical Systems

Comprehensive electrical system diagnostics and repair, including batteries, alternators, and starters.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance services to prevent problems and extend the life of your vehicle.
